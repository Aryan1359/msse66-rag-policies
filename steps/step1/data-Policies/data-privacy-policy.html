Data Privacy Policy

Filename: data-privacy-policy.html

DocID: PRIVACY-008

Effective Date: Jan 1, 2025

Last Updated: Sep 15, 2025

Purpose

To ensure the protection and responsible handling of personal and sensitive data.

Scope

Applies to all employees and contractors who collect, store, or process data.

Data Types

  • Personally Identifiable Information (PII)
  • Health and financial data
  • Customer and employee records

Handling Guidelines

  • Data must be encrypted in transit and at rest.
  • Access is granted on a need-to-know basis.
  • Retention periods must comply with legal requirements.

Rights and Requests

Individuals may request access, correction, or deletion of their data via privacy@company.com.
