IT Security Policy

Filename: it-security-policy.html

DocID: ITSEC-007

Effective Date: Jan 1, 2025

Last Updated: Sep 15, 2025

Purpose

To protect company data, systems, and networks from unauthorized access and cyber threats.

Scope

Applies to all employees, contractors, and third-party vendors accessing company systems.

Authentication

  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for all logins.
  • Passwords must be changed every 90 days.

Device Security

  • Company devices must be encrypted and password-protected.
  • Personal devices may not be used to access sensitive systems.

Incident Reporting

Security incidents must be reported within 24 hours to security@company.com.

Prohibited Actions

  • Installing unauthorized software
  • Sharing login credentials
  • Disabling antivirus or firewall tools
