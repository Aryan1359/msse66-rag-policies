Benefits Overview

Filename: benefits-overview.pdf

DocID: BENEFITS-013

Effective Date: Jan 1, 2025

Last Updated: Sep 15, 2025

Purpose

To summarize the benefits available to eligible employees.

Scope

Applies to all full-time employees and part-time employees working 20+ hours/week.

Health Insurance

- Medical, dental, and vision coverage through UnitedHealth

- Coverage begins on the first day of the month following hire

- Dependent coverage available

Retirement

- 401(k) with 5% company match

- Vesting begins after 1 year of service

Leave

- Paid Time Off (PTO)

- Parental Leave: 8 weeks paid

- Bereavement Leave: 5 days

Other Benefits








- Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

- Tuition reimbursement up to $3,000/year

- Gym membership discount

Contact

For benefits questions, email benefits@company.com.
